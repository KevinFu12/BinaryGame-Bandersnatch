About the Film

SYNOPSIS

The Binary Games follows Alif, a lazy but brilliant computer science student whose ordinary life takes a sharp turn when he receives a mysterious text offering $1.5 billion in exchange for playing a “game.” What starts as a curious tap on his phone soon plunges him into a high-stakes virtual deathmatch—where every coding challenge could mean life or death. Trapped in a sinister digital world run by a masked figure known only as the Frontman, Alif must outthink, outcode, and outlast hundreds of competitors in real-time programming trials, SQL puzzles, and debugging nightmares. But as players begin to vanish and gunshots echo through their calls, Alif realizes the price of failure isn’t just losing—it’s elimination.
